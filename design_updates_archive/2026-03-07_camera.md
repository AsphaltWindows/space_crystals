# Design Update: Camera

**Date**: 2026-03-07
**Files created**: `design/camera.md`

## Summary

New design file establishing camera and viewport behavior.

## Decisions

- **Fixed zoom**: Camera displays exactly 28 GridUnits horizontally. No zoom in/out controls.
- **Vertical coverage**: Determined by screen aspect ratio minus HUD vertical space (top resource bar + bottom info/selection/command panel).
- **HUD layout**: Resource bar at top, info/selection/command panel at bottom. No side panels.
- **Panning**: Not yet designed (deferred to future session).
